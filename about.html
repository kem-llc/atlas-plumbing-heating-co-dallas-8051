Our story

Your Trusted Local Plumber in Dallas Since 1985

To provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with reliable, honest, and affordable plumbing and heating services, delivered with integrity and exceptional craftsmanship.

Atlas Plumbing & Heating Co was founded in 1985 with a simple mission: deliver quality plumbing services with old-fashioned customer service. What started as a one-man operation has grown into a trusted team of licensed professionals serving the entire Dallas area. We've built our reputation on reliability, transparency, and a commitment to doing the job right the first time.

Over the decades, we've helped thousands of Dallas residents with everything from minor faucet repairs to major water heater installations and emergency pipe replacements. We're proud to be a family-owned business that treats every customer like a neighbor. Our team stays up-to-date with the latest technology and techniques to provide efficient, lasting solutions.
