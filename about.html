Our story

Dallas’s Trusted Plumbing Experts Since 1979

To provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with reliable, honest plumbing services that exceed expectations – every time.

Atlas Plumbing & Heating Co was founded in 1979 with a simple mission: deliver quality plumbing work with integrity. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Dallas’s most trusted plumbing companies, serving thousands of customers across the metroplex. Our longevity is built on consistent craftsmanship, transparent pricing, and a commitment to doing the job right.

Today, we continue that tradition with a team of licensed, experienced plumbers who treat every home like their own. We invest in the latest tools and training to provide efficient solutions, from emergency repairs to full system installations. As a local, family-owned business, we’re proud to be part of the Dallas community and to earn your trust one job at a time.
