Our story

Dallas's Trusted Plumbing Experts Since 1952

To provide Dallas homeowners and businesses with reliable, honest, and high-quality plumbing services — delivered with integrity and respect.

Atlas Plumbing & Heating Co was founded in 1952 with a simple mission: deliver dependable plumbing services with old-fashioned work ethic. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Dallas's most trusted plumbing companies, serving thousands of customers across the metroplex. Our longevity is built on a commitment to quality craftsmanship, transparent pricing, and treating every home like our own.

Today, we continue that tradition with a team of licensed, background-checked technicians who undergo continuous training. We invest in the latest diagnostic tools and techniques to provide efficient, lasting repairs. Whether it's a routine drain cleaning or a complex repipe, we approach every job with the same dedication that has kept our customers coming back for over 70 years.
