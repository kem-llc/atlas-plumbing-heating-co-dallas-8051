Drain Cleaning & Repair

Professional drain cleaning, sewer line repair, and clog removal.

Schedule this service

Overview

Slow drains and recurring clogs are more than an inconvenience — they can signal deeper issues in your plumbing system. Atlas Plumbing uses advanced hydro-jetting and video inspection to clear blockages and identify problems like tree root intrusion or pipe damage. We provide thorough cleaning and lasting repairs to restore proper flow and prevent future backups.

What's included

  • Hydro-jetting for stubborn clogs
  • Video camera inspection for accurate diagnosis
  • Sewer line repair and replacement
  • Eco-friendly cleaning options

How it works

1

Step 1: Video Inspection

We insert a high-definition camera into your drain to locate the blockage and assess pipe condition.

2

Step 2: Hydro-Jetting or Snaking

Depending on the clog, we use either a powerful hydro-jet or a motorized auger to clear the line completely.

3

Step 3: Post-Clearing Verification

We re-inspect the line to confirm full flow and recommend any necessary repairs or maintenance.

Common questions

How often should I have my drains cleaned?

We recommend professional drain cleaning every 1-2 years for most homes, or more frequently if you have older pipes or recurring clogs.

Will hydro-jetting damage my pipes?

No, hydro-jetting uses high-pressure water that is safe for all types of pipes, including PVC and cast iron. It effectively removes buildup without chemicals.
