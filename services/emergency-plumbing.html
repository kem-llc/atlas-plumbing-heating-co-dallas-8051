Emergency Plumbing

24/7 emergency response for burst pipes, severe clogs, gas leaks, and more.

Schedule this service

Overview

When a plumbing emergency strikes, every minute counts. Atlas Plumbing provides round-the-clock emergency services throughout Dallas. Our rapid-response team is always on standby, ready to handle any crisis — from flooded basements to broken water mains. We arrive fully equipped to assess, contain, and repair the issue quickly, preventing further damage to your property.

What's included

  • Available 24/7 including holidays
  • 60-minute average response time
  • Licensed and insured emergency technicians
  • Free phone consultation to assess urgency

How it works

1

Step 1: Call & Assess

Call our emergency line. Our dispatcher will ask key questions to determine the severity and send the nearest available technician.

2

Step 2: Rapid Arrival & Diagnosis

Our plumber arrives within 60 minutes, assesses the situation, and provides a clear estimate before any work begins.

3

Step 3: Repair & Cleanup

We perform the repair using high-quality parts, then clean the work area thoroughly. We'll also advise on preventive measures.

Common questions

How much does an emergency plumbing call cost?

Our emergency service fee is $99, which covers the after-hours dispatch. Repair costs are quoted separately based on the work needed, and we always provide an upfront estimate.

Do you handle gas line emergencies?

Yes, our technicians are trained and certified to handle gas leaks and gas line repairs. If you smell gas, leave your home immediately and call us from outside.
