Leak repair, pipe replacement, and whole-home repiping services.

Overview

Leaky or corroded pipes can cause water damage, mold, and high utility bills. Atlas Plumbing provides expert pipe repair and full repiping solutions for Dallas homes. Whether you need a single section replaced or a complete upgrade to modern PEX or copper, our team works efficiently to minimize disruption. We use durable materials and proven techniques to ensure your plumbing system is reliable for years to come.

What's included

  • Leak detection and repair
  • Whole-home repiping with minimal wall damage
  • Copper and PEX piping options
  • Warranty on all pipe work

Common questions

How do I know if I need repiping?

Signs include frequent leaks, low water pressure, discolored water, or pipes that are over 50 years old. A video inspection can confirm the condition.

How long does a full repipe take?

For an average home, repiping typically takes 2-5 days, depending on the size of the house and the complexity of the layout.
