Water Heater Services

Installation, repair, and maintenance of all water heater types.

Overview

Whether you need a new water heater installed or your existing one repaired, Atlas Plumbing & Heating Co has you covered. We work with all major brands and types, including tankless, gas, electric, and hybrid models. Our team will help you choose the most energy-efficient option for your home and budget. We also offer routine maintenance to extend the life of your unit and prevent breakdowns.

  • Energy-efficient options to lower bills
  • Warranty on parts and labor

Common questions

How long does a water heater installation take?

Most installations are completed in one day. Tankless units may require additional time for gas line or venting modifications.

What size water heater do I need?

It depends on your household size and usage. We provide a free assessment to determine the right capacity.
