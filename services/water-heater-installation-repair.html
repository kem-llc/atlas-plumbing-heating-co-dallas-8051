Overview

A reliable water heater is essential for comfort. Atlas Plumbing installs, repairs, and maintains all major brands of tank and tankless water heaters. We help you select the right size and energy-efficient model to meet your household’s needs, and our work is backed by a warranty for your peace of mind.

Common questions

Should I choose a tank or tankless water heater?

Tankless heaters save energy and space but have higher upfront costs. We’ll help you compare based on your home’s needs.

How long does installation take?

Most installations are completed in 2–4 hours, depending on the system and complexity.
