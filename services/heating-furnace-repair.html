Overview

When the temperature drops, you need a reliable heating system. Atlas Plumbing & Heating Co provides comprehensive heating services, including furnace repair, installation, and maintenance. We work with gas, electric, and heat pump systems. Our technicians are trained to diagnose and fix issues quickly, ensuring your home stays warm and comfortable all winter long.

Common questions

How do I know if I need a new furnace?

Signs include frequent repairs, uneven heating, rising energy bills, or a unit over 15 years old. We can help you decide.

Do you offer maintenance plans?

Yes, we offer seasonal maintenance plans to keep your heating system running efficiently and catch issues early.
